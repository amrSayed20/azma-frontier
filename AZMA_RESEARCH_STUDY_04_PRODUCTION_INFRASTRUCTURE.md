# AZMA Research Study 04 - Production AI Infrastructure

## Study Status

This is a constitutional research study artifact only.

No implementation, no benchmarking, no ranking, no winner selection, and no adoption recommendation are included.

## Objective

Build a comprehensive high-level constitutional knowledge base of the production infrastructure required to operate a sovereign, provider-agnostic, multimodal AI Operating System.

## Domain Classification

### 1) GPU Execution Architectures
- Purpose: Execute multimodal inference/training workloads on accelerated compute.
- General strengths: High compute throughput and modality support.
- General weaknesses: Cost intensity and operational complexity.
- Operational role: Core compute substrate.
- Enterprise suitability: High.
- Suitability for AZMA OS: Critical.
